Budget App
It is implemented in Flutter, available on:

Budget is an application that will help you to analyze your spend and get statistics of your transactions income or outcome between your wallets. Also there are others funcionalities like mobile usage and remaiden mobile data that will help you to manage others aspect of your life.

Clear interface:

Budget expense income and transfers between wallets tracker, money, finances app is very easy to use: you can quickly add a transaction with just a couple of clicks.

Illustrative display:

The app will automatically draw up current balance and create a pictorial diagram showing your spending patterns (expenses and income and transfers).

Explications:

Check out detailed reports in custom periods of time and each category of operation, or type - whatever works best for you.

Multicurrency:

The app supports various currencies and show real-time exchange rates which provides comfort of use while traveling abroad, or you can set your rate manually if you are more confortable with that in case you receive income in foreign currencies, etc.

Safety:

It's optional but you can set a biometic fingerprint to protect data on your budget so that only you have access to this important information.

What's included?
  • Create unlimited budgets on the mobile app or on desktop.
  • Sync budget data across all devices becuase the data is storage in the cloud.
  • Personalize categories and line items in a way that makes sense for you.
  • Adjust or reset your budget throughout the month.
  • Stay on top of spending.
  • Manually track transactions.
  • Set up savings funds and track progress along the way.
  • Check your budget from anywhere.
  • Calculate the reminden of your mobile data
